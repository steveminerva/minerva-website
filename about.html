About Us

Minerva & Morand Motors

A joint venture of excellence

Minerva Motors & Cie — the historic Belgian marque founded in Antwerp in 1897 — returns in partnership with Morand, the Swiss Formula 1 and Le Mans engineering team based in Cousset. Together they unite Belgian craft and heritage with contemporary Formula 1 engineering.

The project unfolds as a sequence of three motor cars. The Aegis Racing, a circuit-only V12 hypercar that establishes the marque's engineering authority; the Aegis GT, the first road-homologated Minerva; and the Sovereign — the world's first hydrogen-hydride V12 luxury SUV, hand-assembled in Antwerp.

Across chassis, energy management, hydrogen and design, Minerva is engineered by specialists who operate at the highest level of their disciplines — a venture conceived, from the first line, as a pursuit of excellence. Sapientia et Ars.

The Partners

Design — Kortrijk & Turin

Granstudio

The design house of Lowie Vermeersch — former Pininfarina Design Director — author of the form of every Minerva, from the Aegis to the Sovereign.

Engineering — LMP1 & LMP2 · 24 Hours of Le Mans

Morand Cars

The Swiss endurance team competing in the World (WEC) and European (ELMS) championships, and a Le Mans engineering team. Morand engineers the chassis architecture and active suspension across the Aegis and Sovereign, and assembles the Aegis in Switzerland — Swiss Made.

Powertrain — V12 OEM

Italtecnica

The OEM engine house behind the Minerva V12 — the naturally-aspirated V12 NA and the hydrogen V12 H₂ — heir to the Ferrari-derived V12 of the Maserati MC12 and fifteen years of Peugeot Sport motorsport.

Energy & Hydrogen

belinus

belinus Energy supplies the marque's proprietary HydraCell hydrogen production and graphene supercapacitor technology — powering the Sovereign's V12 H₂ and the Morand active suspension, and delivering true energy sovereignty to its owners.

Haute Joaillerie — Angoulême

Charles Fontana & Cie

Maître Joaillier crafting each Minerva's analogue fascia clock and precious-metal commission detailing — titanium, platinum and gold, archived and signed for every motor car.

Crystal — Belgian Royal Warrant

Val Saint-Lambert

Belgium's Royal Warrant crystal house. Each Sovereign carries a rear-cabin crystal service, hand-engraved with the motor car's individual commission number.

Leather — Brussels, est. 1829

Delvaux

The world's oldest luxury leather house, founded in Brussels in 1829. Delvaux dresses the Minerva cabin in full-grain leather and composes the bespoke luggage commission for each vehicle.

Technology — DC Charging & Manufacturing Automation

Evonity

Evonity delivers DC charging infrastructure and manufacturing automation engineering through its automotive-manufacturing specialist team.

Tracking & Traceability Solutions

Eveatec

A real-time location and auto-identification specialist — RTLS, RFID and sensing — underpinning Minerva's component traceability and supply-chain visibility from atelier to delivery.
