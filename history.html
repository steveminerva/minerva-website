Heritage — Since 1897

HISTORY

From a bicycle workshop in Antwerp to the courts of Europe — Minerva was, for a generation, among the most accomplished names in motoring.

1897

Foundation — Antwerp

Sylvain de Jong incorporates Minerva

Founded in Antwerp, Minerva begins as a maker of bicycles and motorised cycles before turning its precision toward the motor car.

1902

First Automobiles

The first Minerva motor cars

Minerva's earliest automobiles establish a reputation for refined engineering — the beginning of a marque defined by silence, power and craft.

1908

Innovation — The Silent Knight

The Knight sleeve-valve engine

Minerva adopts the Knight sleeve-valve engine, prized for its near-silent running. Smoothness and quietude become the marque's signature — and its competitive edge.

1910s

The Marque of Kings

Coachbuilt for the courts of Europe

Minerva becomes a carriage of royalty and heads of state — with documented clientele across the courts of Belgium, Sweden and Norway. Its name stands among the era's most prestigious motor cars.

1920s

The Golden Age

Grand coachbuilt motor cars

The AK, AC and the formidable AL — large, silent, exquisitely coachbuilt — place Minerva alongside Rolls-Royce and Hispano-Suiza as a definitive luxury automobile.

1930s

Depression & Decline

The luxury market contracts

The global depression bears down on the great luxury houses. Production of Minerva's grand cars winds down as the market for coachbuilt motor cars collapses.

1956

Dormancy

The marque falls silent

After post-war years of licensed and military production, automobile manufacture under the Minerva name ceases. The marque enters a long dormancy — its legacy preserved in collections and concours.

2023

Renaissance Begins

A new generation takes shape

Technical development begins on a new-generation hypercar, alongside hydrogen-related innovations destined to be integrated within the marque — laying the engineering foundations for Minerva's return.

2027

The Reawakening

Beyond luxury and innovation. The marque of kings returns.

130 years after its incorporation, Minerva reawakens — uniting its founding pursuit of silence and craft with Formula 1 engineering and a new generation of powertrains.
