Minerva Motors Heritage

The Minerva Luxury Motors Club preserves the marque's documented record — period photographs, coachbuilders' records and chassis registers — in close partnership with existing stakeholders dedicated to the conservation of the brand. We invite owners, historians and enthusiasts to take part in conserving this heritage, and to join us at our collector's events.
