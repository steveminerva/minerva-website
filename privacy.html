Scope. This Policy explains how Minerva Holding B.V. processes personal data of visitors and registrants of this website. It is drafted to address the requirements of the EU General Data Protection Regulation (GDPR), the UK GDPR and Data Protection Act 2018, and the Swiss Federal Act on Data Protection (FADP).

1Who we are

This website is operated by Minerva Holding B.V. ("Minerva", "we", "us"). Minerva Holding B.V. is the data controller responsible for the personal data processed through this site. For all data-protection matters you may contact us at legal@minervaluxurymotors.com.

Where required, Minerva Holding B.V. will designate a representative and/or data-protection point of contact for the European Union, the United Kingdom and Switzerland. Requests to identify the relevant representative may be sent to the address above.

2The laws that apply

Depending on your place of residence, the following frameworks apply to our processing of your personal data:

EU · GDPR UK · UK GDPR / DPA 2018 CH · FADP (revFADP)

Where these frameworks differ, we apply the standard that affords you the highest level of protection. References below to "applicable data-protection law" mean whichever of the above applies to you.

3The personal data we collect

We collect only the data necessary for the purposes described in this Policy:

  • Subscription / announcement updates: your email address.
  • Archive interest registration: your email address and the interests you select (history conservation, collector's events).
  • Account & order tracking (Login): identification and contact details and information relating to your order or commission.
  • Communications: the content of messages you send to us, including to legal@minervaluxurymotors.com.
  • Technical & usage data: information collected through cookies and similar technologies (see Section 7), subject to your consent where required.

4Why we process it, and our legal bases

We process personal data for the following purposes, relying on the legal bases indicated:

  • To send you announcements and updates you requested — your consent (GDPR/UK GDPR Art. 6(1)(a); FADP consent), which you may withdraw at any time.
  • To register and manage your interest in the archives and events — your consent.
  • To operate your account and let you track an order or commission — performance of a contract or steps taken at your request (Art. 6(1)(b)).
  • To secure, maintain and improve the website — our legitimate interests (Art. 6(1)(f)), balanced against your rights.
  • To comply with legal obligations — compliance with a legal obligation (Art. 6(1)(c)).

5Marketing & your choices

We send marketing communications only where you have given permission, or as otherwise permitted by applicable law. You can withdraw your consent or opt out at any time, without affecting the lawfulness of processing carried out before withdrawal, by contacting legal@minervaluxurymotors.com or using the unsubscribe mechanism in our messages.

6Sharing & international transfers

We share personal data only with service providers acting on our behalf (for example, hosting and email delivery), with professional advisers, or where required by law. These recipients are bound by appropriate confidentiality and data-protection obligations.

Where personal data is transferred outside the EEA, the UK or Switzerland, we rely on a lawful transfer mechanism — such as the European Commission's Standard Contractual Clauses, the UK International Data Transfer Addendum, and the recognition mechanisms of the Swiss FADP — together with any additional safeguards required.

7Cookies & similar technologies

We use cookies and similar technologies in the following categories:

  • Strictly necessary — required for the website to function; always active.
  • Analytics — help us understand how the site is used; set only with your consent.
  • Marketing — used to personalise communications; set only with your consent.

On your first visit, a consent banner lets you accept all, reject non-essential cookies, or set your preferences. You can change your choices at any time using the "Cookie settings" link in the website footer. Non-essential cookies are not set before you consent.

8How long we keep your data

We retain personal data only for as long as necessary for the purposes set out in this Policy, or as required to meet legal, accounting or reporting obligations. When data is no longer needed, we delete or anonymise it.

9Your rights

Subject to applicable data-protection law, you have the right to access, rectify, erase, restrict or object to the processing of your personal data, to data portability, and to withdraw consent at any time. To exercise any right, contact legal@minervaluxurymotors.com.

You also have the right to lodge a complaint with a supervisory authority:

  • EU — the data-protection authority of your country of residence or work.
  • UK — the Information Commissioner's Office (ICO).
  • Switzerland — the Federal Data Protection and Information Commissioner (FDPIC).

10Security

We implement appropriate technical and organisational measures to protect personal data against unauthorised access, loss or misuse. No method of transmission or storage is completely secure, and we cannot guarantee absolute security.

11Children

This website is not directed to children, and we do not knowingly collect personal data from children. If you believe a child has provided us with personal data, please contact legal@minervaluxurymotors.com.

12Changes to this Policy

We may update this Policy from time to time. The "Last updated" date above indicates when it was last revised. Material changes will be communicated where required by applicable law.
